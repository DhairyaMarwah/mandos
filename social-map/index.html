Mandos shows friends and people near you, live. It's a two-way deal: you'll see others on the map only while you're sharing your own location. Stop sharing and you go invisible — and the map hides everyone else from you too.

  • Others see only your approximate area + last-active time — never your exact spot.
  • Location is read only when the map is open — never in the background.
  • Go invisible anytime with Ghost mode — browse events & chat without sharing.
